Journey to Your Destiny

The Alchemist tells the mystical story of Santiago, an Andalusian shepherd boy who yearns to travel in search of a worldly treasure. His quest will lead him to riches far different—and far more satisfying—than he ever imagined.

Santiago's journey teaches us about the essential wisdom of listening to our hearts, recognizing opportunity, and following our dreams. The story has inspired people all over the world to live their dreams and follow their destiny.

Along the way, he meets a Gypsy woman, a man who calls himself king, and an alchemist, all of whom point Santiago in the direction of his quest. No one knows what the treasure is, or if Santiago will be able to surmount the obstacles in his path. But what starts out as a journey to find worldly goods turns into a discovery of the treasure found within.

About Paulo Coelho

Paulo Coelho de Souza is a Brazilian lyricist and novelist who has become one of the most widely read authors in the world. His books have sold more than 225 million copies worldwide and have been translated into more than 80 languages.

Born in Rio de Janeiro in 1947, Coelho's journey to becoming a writer was not straightforward. Before dedicating his life to literature, he worked as a director, theater actor, songwriter, and journalist. His breakthrough came when, at the age of 38, he experienced a spiritual awakening during his pilgrimage along the Road to Santiago de Compostela in Spain.

The Alchemist, published in 1988, established Coelho as a world-renowned author. The book's profound messages about following one's dreams and listening to one's heart have resonated with readers of all ages across the globe.
